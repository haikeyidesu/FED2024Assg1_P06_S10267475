Fitness Product Survey

Hello! We're a group of first-year students from Ngee Ann Polytechnic! We are currently working on an project to design a fitness product that improves on the existing ones out there!

As part of our research, we are conducting a survey to better understand the fitness habits, preferences and needs of users of fitness products, in hope of developing insights that would allow us to provide a better fitness product!

Don't worry your details collected remain anonymous and will only be used for the ideation of the product and nothing more!

Thank you for your time and kind participation!
